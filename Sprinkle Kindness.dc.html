A LITTLE ABOUT ME

Hi, I'm so glad you're here!

I'm 21 and I absolutely love people — baking is simply how I get to share a little joy. I hand-decorate sugar cookies for the moments that matter most, and I pour just as much care into how they taste as how they look.

So many sugar cookies are far too sweet. Mine are made to be just right — beautiful to look at, and a genuine delight to eat.

Someday, Lord willing, I dream of opening a bakery of my own. Until then, thank you for following along and letting me sprinkle a little kindness your way.
